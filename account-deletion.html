Account Deletion

House Party users have the right to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Deletion

You may request account deletion by emailing us from the email address associated with your account:

Email: support@houseparty.app

What Happens Next

  • Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted
  • Requests are processed within 7 days
  • Some data may be retained temporarily if required by law

If you have questions, please contact us using the email above.
